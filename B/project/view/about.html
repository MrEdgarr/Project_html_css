Introduction to Aquarium



Aquarium is one of the great tourist sites. Confirmed to be the largest aquarium in the world, this place is the "home" of thousands of rare marine creatures. Join us to discover interesting things in the aquarium right now.

Aquarium opened in November 2016, is the largest artificial aquarium in the world. When you come here, you will admire the beautiful ocean world with 100,000 marine creatures of 800 different species and 20,000 species of colorful corals. In addition, the aquarium in Singapore also has the longest lazy river in the world, about 620 meters long.

Aquarium Aquarium has 10 artificial ocean zones designed to simulate 49 different seas in the world such as Southeast Asia, Karimata Strait, Java Sea, Malacca Strait, Belgan Bay, Red Sea and other regions. distant sea and other interesting.
